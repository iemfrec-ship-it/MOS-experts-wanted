SecureForce Ltd. — Incident Report #A-114

Site: Hartwell Business Park, Building C  |  Date: Sunday 14 April — 23:47  |  Officer: D. Kowalski #2281

At approximately 23:47, during a routine patrol of the car park on the north side of Building C, Officer Kowalski noticed that the side entrance door was ajar. The door, which should be locked after 20:00, showed no signs of forced entry. Officer Kowalski immediately radioed the control room and waited for backup before entering the building.

Once inside, the officers found that the storage room on the ground floor had been accessed. Several boxes had been moved and a laptop was reported missing by the site manager, who was contacted at 00:15. No individuals were found on the premises. CCTV footage from cameras 4 and 7 was reviewed — both cameras had been manually disabled between 22:30 and 23:50.

The site manager confirmed that only three members of staff hold a key to the storage room. Their details have been passed to the investigation team. The police were called at 00:20 and arrived on site at 00:52. The entrance door has been secured and the incident has been logged in the site register.

Status: Under Investigation

Site: Norfield Shopping Centre, Main Entrance  |  Date: Saturday 20 April — 14:15  |  Officer: T. Barnes #1047

At 14:15 on Saturday afternoon, Officer Barnes was stationed at the main entrance of Norfield Shopping Centre when a member of the public approached and reported that a woman had collapsed near the food court on the first floor. Officer Barnes immediately contacted the control room, requested first aid assistance, and proceeded to the location.

Upon arrival, Officer Barnes found a woman in her sixties lying on the floor, conscious but disoriented. A first aider arrived within three minutes and began assessment. Officer Barnes secured a two-metre perimeter around the casualty to prevent crowding and ensure clear access for emergency services.

An ambulance was called at 14:19 and arrived at 14:31. The casualty was taken to hospital. Officer Barnes completed a full written report and submitted it to the site manager before the end of his shift. No suspicious activity was recorded in connection with the incident.

Status: Closed
